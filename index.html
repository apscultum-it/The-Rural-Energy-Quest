The Rural Energy Quest

One valley. Four seasons. Forty-eight decisions that connect homes, farms, residues and community power.

Riverstone Valley has one year to reduce waste, protect vulnerable residents and build a credible clean-energy plan. You have been appointed the valley's energy steward. Your decisions will affect the budget, resilience, climate impact and community trust.
